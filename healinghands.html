Case Study 1: The Healing Hands App

Watch Where You Put Your Hands!

The Healing Hands help you heal with the art of Jin Shin Jyutsu by showing you how to help yourself with easy-to-follow pictures and audio guide. If you prefer or need professional help instead, the Healing Hands connect you with professional JSJ therapist near you. Want to learn more about JSJ? You can choose from a large selection of online and in-person classes! Are you a teacher or therapist? We help you connect with patients and students and take care of logistics and administration so you can focus more important things.

WHAT'S THE CHALLENGE

EMPATHIZE AND DEFINE THE PROBLEM

The Healing Hands app is a “Destination” app. Only people in-the-know would search for it. So the assumption is that they know of Jin Shin Jyutsu (JSJ) benefits for their health

WHY aren’t people using JSJ therapy as often even when they know the benefits? There is too much unconsolidated and uncurated information on the JSJ therapy. It is simply inconvenient to use.

WHAT IF WE COULD

help remove that barrier but curating and consolidating all that information so that you can conveniently access it anywhere, anytime from the palm of your hand?

Conducting competitive analysis on existing apps and websites was enlightening: being able to see what worked well and what didn’t helped me think about the opportunities I can incorporate and the challenges I have to resolve as I proceed through the design process.

OPPORTUNITIES

      NO KNOWN COMPETITORS
      LOGISTICAL SUPPORT for patients and therapists
      CONVENIENT ACCESS to therapy information, accessible anywhere, anytime
      COMPLEMENTARY THERAPY in conjunction with conventional healthcare
      CONCIERGE SERVICES to accomodate patients who recovering in the hospital

CHALLENGES

      LACK OF INITIAL KNOWLEDGE about Jin Shin Jyutsu therapy may lead to fewer registrations
      ACCESS TO SELF-CARE THERAPY information without WIFI or cellular connection (during transit/flight). Downloadable therapy materials for members must be accessible without connection
      RESISTANCE from current therapists to register and to be verified
      COSTLY professional therapy session can be a deterrent to many patients who are open to the alternative therapy but can not afford it. But if we can engage healthcare providers and insurance companies to understand the benefits of JSJ therapy, the cost challenge may be mitigated.

USER SURVEY and USER INTERVIEW FINDINGS

click here for complete pdf

ATTITUDE

  • Users are highly motivated to find solutions for his or her healthcare.
  • To ensure user conversions, the solution(s) provided by Healing Hands must be easy and CONVENIENT to access, personalized and CURATED with membership, and efficiently connect with therapists near you (CONCIERGE)

NEEDS and GOALS

  • Maintain User Retention by providing information users seek clearly and efficiently. Complete with visual aids.
  • CURATED information with personal subscription to focus on individual healing.
  • CONVENIENCE is key when looking for a therapy.
  • CONCIERGE services to find a professional therapy near you, complete with notification features as appointment reminders and self-care reminders.

PAIN POINTS

  • Privacy and data collection concerns: Healing Hands must BUILD TRUST so subscribers feel confident when using the app.
  • Complicated navigation leads to complaints and eventually loss of user retention.
  • Must be CONVENIENT and easy to fulfill Users’s immediate need.
